Cap'n Proto

Cap'n Proto is a data interchange format and object-capability RPC system that is insanely fast. Think of it like a binary JSON, similar to Protocol Buffers or Thrift. In fact, the author of Cap'n Proto was also the author of version 2 of Google's Protocol Buffers.
